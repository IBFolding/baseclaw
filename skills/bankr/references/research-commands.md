## Red Flags Checklist

Before trading any token, verify:

- [ ] Contract is verified on explorer
- [ ] Can sell/transfer tokens (not honeypot)
- [ ] Liquidity is adequate (>$10k)
- [ ] Liquidity is locked or burned
- [ ] No excessive taxes (>10%)
- [ ] Team is known or doxxed (preferred)
- [ ] Active development
- [ ] Real use case (not just speculation)
- [ ] Healthy holder distribution
- [ ] No recent contract issues
